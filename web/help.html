Authentication Methods

IAM authentication uses AWS SigV4 signing. The Lambda execution role is automatically configured with the required kafka-cluster:* permissions. Simply select "IAM" and provide your bootstrap servers using port 9098.

Store your SCRAM credentials in AWS Secrets Manager and associate the secret with your MSK cluster. Provide the Secret ARN in the configuration. Use port 9096 for SASL/SCRAM.

Create a Private CA in ACM and issue a client certificate. Enable mTLS on your MSK cluster and provide the certificate ARN. Use port 9094 for mTLS connections.

Warning: Plaintext is not recommended for production. Use port 9092. Ensure your MSK cluster has plaintext listeners enabled.

Troubleshooting

  • Connection timeout: Ensure Lambda functions are deployed in a VPC with access to your MSK cluster's security group.
  • Authentication errors: Verify the correct port for your auth method (9092=plaintext, 9094=mTLS, 9096=SCRAM, 9098=IAM).
  • Topic not found: Check that the topic exists and the Lambda role has permission to describe/access it.
